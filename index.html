🌱 Welcome to Deforestation Awareness 🌳

Our planet’s forests are disappearing at an alarming rate. This site aims to spread awareness about deforestation—its causes, effects, and what we can do to protect the lungs of our Earth.

🌍 Purpose

This project was made to help people understand the importance of forests and the impact of deforestation on our world. It encourages awareness and positive change toward sustainability.

🔥 Causes of Deforestation

Logging Industry

Logging for timber and paper products removes massive forest areas. Unsustainable practices lead to habitat destruction and loss of biodiversity.

Agricultural Expansion

Forests are cleared for crops and livestock, especially palm oil and soy. This accounts for over 70% of tropical deforestation.

Urbanization

Growing cities and infrastructure projects consume forest land, replacing natural habitats with concrete structures.

💀 Effects of Deforestation

Climate Change

Deforestation increases CO₂ emissions and reduces oxygen production, accelerating global warming and disturbing the Earth's climate balance.

Biodiversity Loss

Millions of species lose their homes due to deforestation, many becoming endangered or extinct.

Soil Degradation

Tree roots protect the soil from erosion. Without them, land becomes barren, leading to desertification.

🌱 Why It Matters

Forests sustain life

Forests provide clean air, regulate water cycles, and support countless species—including humans. Protecting them means protecting our future.

Human health connection

Healthy forests reduce air pollution and the spread of zoonotic diseases from wildlife to humans.
