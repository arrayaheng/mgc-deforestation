About This Project

This project was created to raise awareness about deforestation and its global impact. It combines research, interactive elements, and visuals to help people understand why forests matter and how deforestation affects our planet. The goal is to inspire environmental responsibility among students and the public.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate visitors about the causes, effects, and urgency of deforestation. By presenting both information and interactive content, the website aims to motivate everyone to take part in sustainable solutions such as reforestation, responsible consumption, and climate action.

Causes of Deforestation

Clearing forests to grow crops and raise livestock.

Agriculture drives about 80% of global deforestation. Forests are burned or cleared to plant soy, palm oil, or to create grazing land for cattle, especially in tropical regions like the Amazon and Southeast Asia.

Effects of Deforestation

Trees protect soil and regulate water cycles.

Why It Matters

Forests are essential for clean air, water, and a stable climate. They sustain life, store carbon, and maintain ecological balance. Protecting them means protecting ourselves, our future, and every living being sharing this planet.
