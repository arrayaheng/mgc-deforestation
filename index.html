Why It Matters

Forests cover nearly 31% of the planet's land area and provide essential services ‚Äî absorbing carbon dioxide, producing oxygen, and maintaining biodiversity. Yet, every year, about 10 million hectares of forest are lost due to human activity.

This loss contributes to 20% of global carbon emissions, worsens climate change, and disrupts the lives of indigenous communities who depend on forests for food and culture.
