About This Project

This project was created to raise awareness about deforestation and its global impact. It combines research, interactivity, and visuals to help people understand the importance of forests and the consequences of deforestation. The goal is to inspire environmental responsibility and sustainable living.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate people about the causes, effects, and urgency of deforestation. By combining facts with interactive elements, it encourages everyone to contribute to sustainable actions such as reforestation, eco-friendly habits, and responsible consumption.
