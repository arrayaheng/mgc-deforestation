Welcome

Forests cover about 31% of the Earth’s land area, but we are losing them at an alarming rate. This website explains the causes, problems, and solutions to deforestation.

Causes of Deforestation

Click below to reveal the main drivers:

🌾 Large areas are cleared to make way for crops like soy, palm oil, and cattle ranching.

🪵 Trees are cut down for timber, paper, and fuel, often unsustainably.

🏗️ Expanding cities and infrastructure destroy natural habitats.

🔥 Some are natural, but many are man-made to clear land quickly.

Problems Caused by Deforestation

Click below to reveal the consequences:

🌍 Trees absorb carbon dioxide; without them, greenhouse gases increase.

🐒 Millions of species lose their homes and face extinction.

💧 Forests help regulate rainfall; without them, droughts and floods worsen.

🌪️ Without tree roots, soil washes away, reducing fertility.

Solutions to Deforestation

Click below to reveal what we can do:

🌱 Reforestation and afforestation projects restore lost forests.

📜 Governments must enforce regulations to stop illegal logging.

🛒 Buy certified wood, paper, and food that don’t harm forests.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Educating others and supporting NGOs helps spread change.
