Introduction

Deforestation is the large-scale removal of forests, often to clear land for farming, logging, or cities. Each year, millions of hectares are lost — an area nearly the size of England. Forest loss fuels climate change, harms animals, and impacts people everywhere.

Causes

  • 🌾 Expanding farms for soy, palm oil, and cattle
  • 🪓 Logging for timber and paper
  • 🏗️ Cities, roads, and dams
  • 🔥 Slash-and-burn farming
  • ⛏️ Mining and extraction

Effects

  • 🌍 More CO₂ → faster climate change
  • 🐒 80% of land species live in forests
  • 💧 Water cycle disruption
  • 🏚️ Harm to indigenous communities
  • 🌪️ More floods and landslides

Solutions

  • 🌱 Plant trees and restore forests
  • 🛑 Stronger forest protection laws
  • ♻️ Reuse & recycle wood and paper
  • 🍫 Buy sustainable food (coffee, chocolate, palm oil alternatives)
  • 👩‍🏫 Spread awareness and education
